The colors for the Holo ActionBar (in Android 4.0) are:

		Light Theme	Dark Theme	Light (Dark AB)
		
* The ActionBar background color
ActionBar	#DDDDDD		#222222		#222222
* The background color of stacked tabs
Tabs		#CDCDCD		#111111		#555555
* The background color of the overflow menu
Menu Overflow	#F5F5F5		#282828		#282828
* The highlight color for button/menu clicks
Highlight	#33B5E5		#33B5E5		#33B5E5
* The background of the Contextual ActionBar
CAB		#FFFFFF		#002E3E		#002E3E
